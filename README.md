An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, identify key business trends, evaluate sales team performance, and generate actionable insights for decision-making. The dashboard also includes sales forecasting to support future business planning.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from a retail business to provide stakeholders with a comprehensive view of sales performance across products, countries, and sales teams. The dashboard is designed using business intelligence best practices, enabling users to monitor KPIs, identify high-performing areas, and make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance through key business KPIs.
- Analyze sales trends over time.
- Compare sales performance across countries.
- Evaluate product category and sales team performance.
- Identify top-performing salespeople.
- Forecast future sales based on historical data.
- Provide business insights and recommendations.

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Executive Dashboard

Provides a high-level overview of business performance using key performance indicators and interactive visualizations.

#### KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Boxes Sold
- Total Customers
- Countries Served
- Total Salespeople
- Average Sales per Box

#### Visualizations
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Country
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Sales Team
- Executive Insights

---

### 2️⃣ Sales Performance Analysis

Provides detailed analysis to identify sales drivers and business opportunities.

#### Visualizations
- Top 10 Salespeople
- Sales by Product
- Sales by Product Size
- Sales Performance Overview
- Sales Performance Breakdown (Decomposition Tree)

---

### 3️⃣ Sales Forecast

Supports business planning by forecasting future sales based on historical performance.

#### Visualizations
- Monthly Sales Trend with Forecast
- Quarterly Sales Performance
- Forecast Summary
- Business Recommendations

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Generated over **$18.6M** in total sales during the reporting period.
- Identified the highest-performing country by sales revenue.
- Determined the best-selling product category.
- Evaluated the performance of each sales team.
- Identified top-performing salespeople.
- Analyzed monthly sales trends throughout the year.
- Forecasted future sales to support planning and decision-making.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in high-performing products and markets.
- Replicate strategies used by the highest-performing sales teams.
- Improve sales performance in lower-performing regions.
- Use forecasting results to optimize inventory and resource planning.
- Continuously monitor sales KPIs to support informed business decisions.
